Choccie Brownie

Description

This choccie delight is the strongest way to finish a power dinner. It is a tastefully light dessert while at the same time giving your tastebuds all they wish for. Consume in moderation.

Ingredients

  • Brownie
  • Vanilla Ice-Cream
  • Choccie Secret Syrup

Steps

  1. Place brownie in plate
  2. Plop a dollop of ice-cream on top of brownie
  3. Decorate the ice-cream and brownie with choccie secret syrup

Khachapuri

Description

Khachapuri pour tout le monde. Recipe for a cold winter night. For a tasty and cheesy delight.

Ingredients

  • Khachapuri Bread
  • Delicious Cheese
  • Egg Yolk

Steps

  1. Place Khachapuri bread on plate
  2. Fill Khachapuri bread with molten cheese
  3. Add egg yolk to delicious combination
